# Inkscape

## To make a stripe pattern and apply it to a stroke

Group several objects together to your liking. 

Select that group and in the topbar go to Object > Pattern > Objects to Pattern

Create a stroke using the Pen tool on the left bar.

Select the stroke and go to Fill and Stroke tab (angled brush in right angle) in the window on the right of the drawing space.

Go to Stroke paint tab and click on a little square with diamond pattern (Pattern).

The pattern you made will show up in the list, along with many other included patterns.

Now you can apply scale, orientation, and offset to your pattern on the stroke.
